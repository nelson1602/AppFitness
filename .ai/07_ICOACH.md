# Purpose

This document defines the official specification of the iCoach Engine.

The iCoach Engine is the core intelligence of AppFitness.

It is responsible for transforming medical evaluations, physical assessments, nutrition data, workout history, recovery metrics, and user goals into deterministic, explainable, and evidence-based recommendations.

Every recommendation generated by iCoach must be reproducible, traceable, scientifically supported, and versioned.

No recommendation may rely solely on generative AI.

---

# Core Mission

iCoach exists to help users improve their health safely through deterministic decision making.

Its responsibilities include:

• Physical assessment analysis

• Medical restriction analysis

• Nutrition planning

• Workout planning

• Progress evaluation

• Recovery evaluation

• Goal tracking

• Habit reinforcement

• Risk detection

• Recommendation generation

# AI Instructions

Every AI agent working on AppFitness must treat the iCoach Engine as the most critical subsystem.

Protect determinism.

Protect explainability.

Protect user safety.

Protect historical integrity.

When uncertainty exists, prefer conservative recommendations.
